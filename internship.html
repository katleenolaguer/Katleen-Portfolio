About the Company

Founded in 1936, Abiva Publishing House Inc. is the oldest and one of the most respected educational publishers in the Philippines. For over eight decades, it has stayed true to its mission of "Helping Teachers Help Their Students."

The company produces textbooks, digital resources, and innovative learning materials that support learners from preschool to college. It also provides teacher training, seminars, and workshops to improve educational practices nationwide.

Headquartered in Quezon City with regional offices in Cebu and Davao, Abiva continues to be a trusted partner of schools and educators across the Philippines.
